#Syntax

`~+`, `siglus`, `[%sgls p=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
hints to the interpreter to memoize (cache) the computation 
of `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

~+  p

##Wide form

~+(p)

##Irregular form

None
