[Short description]

#Syntax

`~=`, `sigtis`, `[%sgts p=twig q=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
hints to the interpreter that `q` may produce a noun equal to the
already existing `p`, avoiding duplication.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

~=  p
        q

##Wide form

~=(p q)

##Irregular form

None
