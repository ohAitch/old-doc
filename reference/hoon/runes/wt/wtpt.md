#Syntax

`?@`, `wutpat`, `[%wtpt p=wing q=twig r=twig]` is a synthetic hoon 
that produces `q` if `p` is an atom, `r` otherwise.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

Kingside:

    ?@  p
      q
    r

##Wide form

?@(p q r)

##Irregular form

None
