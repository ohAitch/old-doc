#Syntax

`wutkaz`, `[%wtkz p=tiki q=twig r=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
evaluates `r` if `p` is equal to the bunt for its tile, otherwise
`q` is evaluated.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

?^  p
      q
    r

##Wide form

?^(p q r)

##Irregular form

None
