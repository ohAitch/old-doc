#Syntax

`?:`, `wutcol`, `[%wtcl p=twig q=twig r=twig]` is a natural hoon
that produces `q` if `p` is yes (`&`, `0`), or `r` if `p` is no
(`|`, 1).

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

?:  p
      q
    r

##Wide form

?:(p q r)

##Irregular form

None
