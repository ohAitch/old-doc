#[buccab `$_` %bccb](#bccb)

Default value, bunt

`$_` is a synthetic rune that produces a `%weed`, the default value or [bunt]](), of a given twig. `$_` is one of the four [tile reductions]() in hoon.

##Produces

Tile: `[%weed p=twig]`

##Sample

`p` is a [tile]().

##Tall form

    $_  p

##Wide form

    $_(p)

##Irregular form

    _p
