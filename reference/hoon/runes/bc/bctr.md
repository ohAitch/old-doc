#buctar `$*` %bctr

#Syntax

`$*`, `buctar`, `[%bctr p=tile]` is a synthetic rune that
produces the bunt (default value) for `p` as a compile-time
constant.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

$*  p

##Wide form

$*(p)

##Irregular form

*p
