#buccol `$:` %bccl

Tile autocons

#Syntax

`buccol` is a tile hoon that declares a [tile autocons](),`[p=tile q=tile]`.

Need a better explanation of tile autocons--I think there's a good one that explains the difference between tile and twig autocons.

[Tile]()

##Sample

`p` is a [tile]().
`q` is a [tile]().

##Tall form

$:  p
        q
    ==

##Wide form

$:(p q)

##Irregular form

[p q]
