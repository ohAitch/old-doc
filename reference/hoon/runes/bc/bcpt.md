#Syntax

`$@`, `bucpat`, `[%bcpt p=wing q=tile]` is a (just barely) natural 
hoon that whips (normalizes) wing `p` into tile `q`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

$@  p
    q

##Wide form

$@(p q)

##Irregular form

p@q
