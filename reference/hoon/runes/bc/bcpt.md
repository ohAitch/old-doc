#[bucpat `$@` %bcpt](#bcpt)

Wing to tile

`$@` is a natural rune that whips (normalizes) wing `p` into tile `q`.

##Produces

Tile: `[%bcpt p=wing q=tile]`

##Sample

`p` is a [`++wing`]().
`q` is a [tile]().

##Tall form

    $@  p
        q

##Wide form

    $@(p q)

##Irregular form

    p@q
