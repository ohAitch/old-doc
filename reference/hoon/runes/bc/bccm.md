#Syntax

`$,`, `buccom`, `[%bccm p=tile]` is a synthetic rune that produces a
normalizing gate (clam) for `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

$,  p

##Wide form

None

##Irregular form

,p

##Examples

++  cord  ,@t                                           ::  text atom (UTF-8)

In ++cord, `,` creates a gate that validates atoms of the odor @t.
