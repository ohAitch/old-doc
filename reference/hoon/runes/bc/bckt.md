Normalizing gate, `%herb`

`$^`, is a tile hoon that declares an [`%herb`](). An `%herb` is a gate, accepting a sample of * and normalizing it as you choose. If you use a twig as a tile, it's treated as an herb.

##Produces

Tile: `[%herb p=twig]`

##Sample

`p` is a [twig]().

##Tall form

    $^  p
            q
        ==

##Wide form

    $^(p q)

##Irregular form

None
