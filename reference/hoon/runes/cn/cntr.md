#Syntax

`%*`, `centar`, `[%cntr p=wing q=twig r=tram]` is a synthetic rune that pull the wing `p` from a tray `q` with changes `r`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

%*  p  q
    p.i.r  q.i.r
    p.i.t.r  q.i.t.r
    ==

##Wide form

%*(p q p.i.r q.i.r, p.i.t.r q.i.t.r)

##Irregular form

None
