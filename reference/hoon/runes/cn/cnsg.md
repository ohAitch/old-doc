#[censig, `%~`, %cnsg](#cnsg)

Pull with sample

`%~` is a synthetic rune that [pull]()s `p` from the [door]() `q` with its sample set to `r`. `%~` is used to evaluate an arm `p` inside of a door `q` with the door's sample set to `r`.

##Produces

Twig: `[%cnsg p=wing q=twig r=twig]`

##Sample

`p` is a [`wing`]()
`q` and `r` are [`twig`]()s.

##Tall form

    %~  p
          q
        r

##Wide form

    %~(p q r)

##Irregular form

    ~(p q r)
