#Syntax

`%~`, `censig`, `[%cnsg p=wing q=twig r=twig]` is a synthetic
hoon that pulls `p` from the door `q` with its sample set to `r`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

%~  p
      q
    r

##Wide form

%~(p q r)

##Irregular form

~(p q r)
