#[cenzey, %cnzy](#cnzy)

Pull limb `p`

`cenzey` is a synthetic internal twig that pulls limb `p` from the subject. `cenzey` is only used internally by `hoon.hoon`.

##Produces

Twig: `[%cnzy p=term]`

##Sample

`p` is a [++term]().
