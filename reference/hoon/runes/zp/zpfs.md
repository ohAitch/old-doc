#Syntax

`!/`, `zapfas`, `[%zpfs p=twig q=twig]` is a natural hoon that should
never be compiled.  When compiled with error checking turned on,
it reports its subject as an error.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

?,  p
        q

##Wide form

?,(p q)

##Irregular form

None
