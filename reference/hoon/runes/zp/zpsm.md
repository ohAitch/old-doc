#Syntax

`!;`, `zapsem`, `[%zpsm p=twig q=twig]` is a natural hoon that
produces the product of twig `q` as a `[type noun]` pair, with
twig `p` defining the type of the type.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

!;  p
        q

##Wide form

!;(p q)

##Irregular form

None
