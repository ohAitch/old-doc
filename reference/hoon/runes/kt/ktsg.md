#Syntax

`^~`, `ketsig`, `[%ktsg p=twig]` is a natural hoon that
tries to execute `p` statically at compile time; if this fails, `p` remains dynamic.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

^~  a

##Wide form

^~(a)

##Irregular form

None
