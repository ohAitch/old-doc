#Syntax

`;;`, `semsem`, `[%smsm p=twig q=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
types `q` as a fixpoint of `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

;;  p
        q

##Wide form

;;(p q)

##Irregular form

None
