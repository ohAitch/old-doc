#Syntax

`=-`, `tishep`, `[%tshp p=twig q=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
pushes `q` on the subject and sends it to `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

=-  p
    q

##Wide form

=-(p q)

##Irregular form

None
