#Syntax

`=.`, `tisdot`, `[%tsdt p=wing q=twig r=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that produces `r` with `p` in the subject set to `q`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

=.  p 
      q
    r

Queenside:

    =.  p  q
    r

##Wide form

=.(p q r)

##Irregular form

None
