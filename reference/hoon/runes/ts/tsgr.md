#Syntax

`=>`, `tisgar`, `[%tsgr p=twig q=twig]` is a natural hoon that
uses the product of `p` as the subject of `q`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

=>  p
    q

##Wide form

=>(p q)
