#Syntax

`=:`, `tiscol`, `[%tscl p=tram q=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that produces `q` with the subject modified by `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

=:  p.i.p      q.i.p
        p.i.t.p    q.i.t.p
        p.i.t.t.p  q.i.t.t.p
      ==
    q
