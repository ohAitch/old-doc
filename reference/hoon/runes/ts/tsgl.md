#Syntax

`=<`, `tisgal`, `[%tsgl p=twig q=twig]` is a synthetic hoon that
uses the product of `q` as the subject of `p`.

##Produces

[Twig or tile]

##Sample

[`p` is a _
`q` is a _]

##Tall form

=<  p
    q

##Wide form

=<(p q)

##Irregular form

p:q
